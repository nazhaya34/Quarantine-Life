In this quarantine period give them time observe them, observe their habbits, observe their likes and dislikes, ask about their life how it is gone, ask about their friends, share your bad and good experiences, appericiate them and give them confidence that you are always at his side wheather his mistake or not, you never leave their side and support them everytime.
